# Add PlanPilot to Your Home Screen

PlanPilot can be installed on your mobile device for a native app-like experience!

## üì± iOS (iPhone/iPad) - Safari

1. Open PlanPilot in **Safari** (this only works in Safari, not Chrome or other browsers on iOS)
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Edit the name if desired (default: "PlanPilot")
5. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner
6. The PlanPilot icon will appear on your home screen!

**Tip**: When you launch PlanPilot from your home screen, it will open in full-screen mode without the Safari browser bars.

## ü§ñ Android - Chrome

1. Open PlanPilot in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **menu** icon (three dots) in the top right corner
3. Select **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
4. Tap **"Add"** or **"Install"** in the popup
5. The PlanPilot icon will appear on your home screen!

**Alternative for Android:**
- You may also see a banner at the bottom of the screen prompting you to "Install PlanPilot"
- Simply tap **"Install"** on that banner
